So, who am I?

I’ve been programming for several years, and over time I’ve developed a strong foundation in front-end and back-end technologies. My main focus is creating fast, responsive, and visually appealing web applications that offer real value to users.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, working on personal projects, and improving my craft. I believe good software isn’t just about code — it’s about creating smooth, simple, and enjoyable experiences.
